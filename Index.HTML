places must visit

prithvi cafe

i had visited in 2018

Quaint little restaurant tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. Blink and You will miss the entrance of the cafe. So be alert enough to spot the black signboard that says "Prithvi cafe" while you are on the main road.The restaurant has reasonably good menu with reasonable prices. The food id delicious , The servings are quite large and some dishes can even be shared by 2 people.Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrities on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid dissapointments . On other days , the crowd there is hep and happening. Hosts the famous"Prithvi theatre fest" , Which you must attend if possible .

jantar mantar-jaipur

i had visited in feb 2019

It's a wonderful place.Must visit this place. Must haired tour guide so it will be better to understand all the things around. It is beautifully maintained small complex with yellow painted walls.It's a historical site famous among the tourists.It contains a number of different devices that were used to measure time in different eras of Indian history.It has a nominal ticket price of 15 rupees for Indians but rupees 220-250 for the foreigners. Nice place to visit . We had an incredible experience visiting this observatory and seeing the accuracy of the astronomical instruments made 300 years ago
